V-CASH

  • Use all products and services at once!

    v-Cash is the payment method used to purchase all the products, services, and
    vouchers we provide. If you charge v-Cash in advance, you can use it for all the
    services easily and conveniently.
    US$1 is exchanged to 10 v-Cash, and it can be charged in units of US$50/200/500/1000. 

    Charge v-Cash

  • Use your v-Cash to keep your classes open as
    much as you want!

    Once you have v-Cash charged, only the exact amount required to keep your class open is used. You only need to make sure you have all students registered in your classes and the appropriate balance of v-Cash to be deducted as you continue focusing on more important tasks, lessening the burden of managing your class. It simply makes teacher's and administration’s life much easier!

    Charge v-Cash

  • Get additional bonus points!

    Top up your v-Cash, and get some points as a bonus!
    Based on the charged amount, 2%(regular charging) or 1%(1 time charging) of
    the amount is given as points, and it can be used for purchasing products and
    vouchers, just like v-Cash. Charge your v-Cash now to use all the products freely.

    Charge v-Cash

VOUCHER

Vouchers are used to pay for particular additional services to add while taking classes.
You can purchase vouchers with v-Cash and/or points. Purchase vouchers to enjoy more professional services.

  • Program Vouchers

    When a new student joins in the middle of a Wings or ELiF class, you can allow the new registration by purchasing this voucher.
    It can be purchased in the units of 5 days/10 days/20 days, which allows a new
    student to choose according to the number of remaining class days until the end of the month.

    Purchase Program Vouchers

  • Lexile Test Vouchers

    The Lexile Test is a test that evaluates and measures students' English reading
    ability, developed by Visang Education in conjunction with MetaMetrics. Students
    who use the English program can objectively assess their levels through the Lexile
    Test, and upgrade their skills by receiving recommendations for English reading
    content that matches the Lexile score. Test your reading skills with Lexile Test Vouchers.

    Purchase Lexile Test Vouchers

  • Training Vouchers

    Vouchers are designed for professional training courses for school/academy
    teachers where they can learn how to install and use programs, teaching techniques, and know-hows. Online Training is provided remotely through web conference tools. Online training is conducted basically in 1 hour. You will be reached by Visang's professional training team once you complete purchase the voucher and submit the desired training schedule, number of attendees.

    Purchase Online Training Voucher

Upgrade your operational skills with Visang's professional training team!

The Visang's professional education team directly visits the local school/academy for on-site training and provides
offline training. It will be a good opportunity to gain various experiences and know-how related to operation and
management from Visang's training. If you submit you desired visit schedule, number of attendees, and desired
training topic through the application page, the head office will check it and get back to you with the schedule and cost.
