Service Notice
Charging v-Cash You can use v-Cash after you purchase it on the website.
Using v-Cash You can use v-Cash at the same value as cash for all the services. (US$1=10cash)
Earn Points Points are given free of charge for charging v-Cash or as an event bonus. If you use standard cash charging, 1% of the amount is saved, and if you use auto charge, 2% of the amount is accumulated.
Using Points Points can be used as v-Cash when paying for basic products or vouchers. (Trial points provided upon school registration are valid for the first 30 days)
v-Cash Refund Remaining v-Cash can be refunded in the same currency used for purchase. The v-Cash purchased with a credit card can be refunded within the first 10 days following the purchase. A commission of 5% will be withheld from the amount of the refund.
Auto Payments for Charging v-Cash Automatic payments are only available with credit cards (Visa, Master, JCB, Amex, UnionPay) and Paypal. Automatic payment cancellation will be effective one month after the automatic payment date. If automatic payments are not made due to changes in payment information, loss of credit cards and mobile phones, or other reasons, service use will be automatically suspended after one month from the last automatic payment date.
