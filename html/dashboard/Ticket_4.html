Notice on the use of vouchers/products
Use of vouchers/products

To use the learning service, you need to charge v-Cash. After the v-Cash has been charged, students are matched with classes on the Admin page. On the first day of learning, the system automatically deducts an amount of v-Cash equivalent to the number of matched students.

Use of vouchers/products
Service Notice
Program Vouchers Pay v-Cash for the vouchers using the integrated management system and use Class Management to select the students to add and apply the vouchers. (1 voucher can be used for 1 student.)
Lexile Test Vouchers Pay v-Cash for the vouchers using the AllviA admin page and use Voucher Management to select the students to provide them to and apply the vouchers. (1 voucher can be used for 1 student.)
Training Vouchers Pay v-Cash for the vouchers using the integrated management system and use Voucher Management to enter the desired learning session date, time, and participants to sign up.

Notice on cancellation/refunds
Notice on cancellation/refunds
Service Notice
Learning Products Cancellation available for up to 3 days after the learning has begun. The refund is paid in points and is equivalent to the remaining unused period of time.
Vouchers Program Vouchers and Lexile Test Vouchers can be canceled only if they haven’t been used. After the purchase, cancellation is not available. Online Training Voucher can be refunded up to 14 days prior to the confirmed training date with Visang team, and Onsite Training Voucher can be refunded up to 60 days prior to the Visang's training team departure date during the confirmed business trip with the Visang's. However, airfare and accommodation fees are non-refundable.
(In addition, vouchers purchased from a reseller cannot be canceled.)
