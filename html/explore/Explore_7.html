Preschool English program based
on AR & MR experiences

Challenge is a digital English learning program
based on AR and MR experiences
that aims
to improve students' balanced reading abilities.

Take advantage of our fun and active curriculum to learn phonics

The use of AR and MR digital content increases students' attention and participation.​ Interacting with
digital characters and learning phonics step by step fosters passion as well as a sense of accomplishment.​

Challenge Content

High-quality learning programs ranging from phonemic awareness through sentence building

Challenge walks students through their studies and ensures they build confidence in English.
Students can develop native-like pronunciation skills through our diverse digital content.

  • Word Learning
  • Key Sentences
  • Sentence Play
  • Reading Play
  • Song Activity
Entertaining MR-based activities that encourage participation

Thanks to Challenge's extensive MR materials, students can be immersed in classes with various exercises that develop
creativity, confidence, and body mobility. This is an excellent and enjoyable approach to master English firsthand.

  • Word Quiz

    Take word quizzes with animated characters

  • Mask Play

    Practice speaking by playing the role of one of the characters in a story

  • Board Game

    Learn English from a board game by creating the alphabet with your body

  • Phonics Game

    Move your body to pop the bubbles while learning phonetic values and vocabulary

  • Music on stage

    Sing and dance along with the characters on the screen

Enjoy unique activities with moving characters in class and at home

In Challenge's AR content, characters come to life and interact with students.
Challenge offers not only 2D but also 3D visuals that move across students’ books and desks.

  • AR Coloring

    The characters you colored come alive

  • AR Reading

    Read stories with characters

  • AR Speaking

    Record and listen to the English conversation between your child and the character

  • AR Camera

    Take photos with the characters you collected

  • AR Quiz

    Play a word review game and find the right pictures for the words

Challenge textbooks boost children's reading and
writing abilities dramatically

1 set of Challenge books contains: Reading Book, Practice Book, Sticker Book, Song Poster,
AR Play Kit (2 sets for 1 year, 1 set per five-month period). The books allow students to independently
examine class materials and practice writing while completing quizzes.

Along with the spiral curriculum, Challenge features three steps

hallenge’s goal is to create systematic reading habits by offering readings, vocabularies, and
exercises along the flow of learning, such as phonemic awareness, phonics, comprehension, and fluency.

  • Step 1

    Ready to Read

    100 words
    60 sentences
    Basic alphabet phonetics
  • Step 2

    Ready with help

    150 words
    90 sentences
    Short vowels, long vowels, consonant digraphs
  • Step 3

    Ready Alone

    200 words
    120 sentences
    Review vowels, consonant blends
