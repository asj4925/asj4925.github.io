Interaction Tool

The interaction tool is always shown on top. Each of the following activity can get sent to the
students which maximize the interaction between teachers and students.

  • ‘Video, Audio, Camera, Pen Tool, Typing’

    activities is provided, and teacher can use this function whenever they need.

  • “Go to More Activities” Button

    When a question has a corresponding More Activity, this button takes you to that specific More Activities page.

  • “Answer” button

    This button appears when the answer is provided by the Teacher’s Guide, or when an example answer is available for the question. The student can check their answers on their devices and edit them if necessary.

  • “Play Video/Audio” buttons

    One of these buttons appears when a video or audio clip is needed to answer the question. You can play the relevant video or audio clip here.

  • “Book” button

    This button appears when an image or passage from the student book or workbook is needed to answer the question. You can check the relevant image or passage here.
