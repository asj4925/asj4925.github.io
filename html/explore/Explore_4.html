Game Activities

  • Unit Game
  • Go Quiz

Two games are provided in each unit to review the words and sentences in the unit.

  • Hungry Bird Game (words)

    Students can review the words and phrases they learned in the Unit by playing the Hungry Bird game. The game can be used before class as a warm-up or after class as a wrap-up.

  • Speed Quiz (sentences)

    Speed Quiz is a sentence completion game that reviews the words and phrases learned in the unit. Students play the game as a team.

Four types of games are provided through Go Quiz. Teachers can use these games for different instructional purposes: to help their students get to know one another or to motivate students to learn words or sentences through competitive ranking games.

  • 2 Truths & 1 Lie

    Students create their own quiz questions and try to guess whether or not their classmates are telling the truth or telling a lie. Selected students write three sentences individually, two of which are true and one of which is a lie sentence.

  • Matching Game (Around the World)

    Students match sybolic items to the nations they represent. The items include local cuisine, capital cities, traditional clothing, famous landmarks, and other cultural icons.

  • Quiz Show

    Quiz Show is a survival word game played for ten rounds. Words are organized into four different categories.

  • True or False

    True or False is a trivia game that is similar to the world famous "Jeopady" game show.
